Prepackaged & Custom Templates

Moror A.I. gives you a range of prepackaged email marketing templates to give your business the visually appealing, professional look and feel it needs. Check out Moror A.I.’s email marketing template examples, then select the template you prefer, draft your message, and automate the send. You can also create custom templates—with no programming experience—that pull in your branding for added impact.

Personalization

Moror A.I. makes it easy to personalize your emails by merging contact attributes into your messages. It’s easy to automatically pull each recipient’s respective name, location, industry, and more into your emails for a personal touch that better engages contacts and prospects alike

Email Lead Scoring

Built-in, automated lead scoring lets you assign scores to leads when they take certain actions, like opening or clicking a link in an email. Set a scoring threshold for lead qualification and only pass leads to sales once their score is high enough. This lets sales teams from small businesses focus more on high quality prospects and frees time previously spent reaching out to dead-end leads.

Tracking and Metrics

Extensive metrics and analytics let you track the ongoing results of your email marketing and provide constant insight into how your efforts are moving the needle. Email campaign metrics—such as open and click-through rates—tell you what’s working and what’s not. Individual contact tracking lets you know which emails specific contacts are engaging with.

Target Your Audience

It’s easy to import lists of contacts and their information into the system for email distribution. You can also add contacts manually or use Moror A.I.’s email finder to trawl the web for the email addresses of existing contacts in your database without one.

Sharing on Social Media

Another one of Moror A.I. great email marketing tools. Include social share buttons in your email templates to let recipients share them out to their social networks. Social media is where people go these days for information, so make it easy for prospects and customers to get the word out about your brand.

Autoresponders

Create and store automatic response emails that get sent when a specified behavioral trigger takes place. For example, set up a welcome email that gets sent automatically when someone signs up for a free trial via one of your web forms. Or an email acknowledging receipt of a customer support inquiry submitted via email.

Real-Time Alerts

Real-time notifications alert you when a specified action takes place, such as a prospect clicking the call-to-action in your email. You’ll see an on-screen popup the moment the action takes place, allowing you to reach out and engage prospects and customers at just the right time.

Track Email Performance

Maintaining insight into the results of your email marketing campaigns is crucially important to improving your performance. Moror A.I. automatically tracks which emails are opened, which ones are clicked, who opens them and the actions leads took on your site after clicking through. You can also receive real time notifications around those metrics.

Lead Scoring and Segmentation

Scoring and segmenting leads lets you accurately qualify sales-ready leads and leave dead-end leads alone. Score leads or add tags based on behaviors they take (e.g. opening an email or visiting a specific webpage) or demographical information they provide you. Maintain insight into leads’ interests and histories so your sales team can close more deals faster.

Email Campaign Analytics

Run reports to see which emails are performing the best (e.g. highest open and click-through rates) and which are coming up short. Understand which are the best times of day to send emails and which to avoid. Don’t settle for average—elevate yourself to exceptional with actionable insights that help continuously improve performance.

Autoresponders

Autoresponders and email tracking go hand in hand. Send automatic email responses based on email tracking results, then further track contact actions and segment even more specifically to increase conversions.

Video Upload and Creation

Upload an existing video for immediate use. Or, create your own using Moror A.I.’s video recording features. Use standard, built-in video themes, or build custom themes to fit your specific needs. Once complete, simply insert your video into an email with an intuitive, drag-and-drop email builder.

Targeted Videos

Boost engagement by sending prospects and customers video emails that align with their interests. Segment your target audience by various attributes—such as job title, industry or company size—and route different videos to different recipients based on those attributes.

Calls to Action

Increase customer interaction with your brand by including a call to action right in the video. Embed a pop up in the video that invites viewers to subscribe to your blog or YouTube channel. Add a comments section to allow viewers to share their experience or ask a question.

Lead Acquisition

Gate important, high-value videos with forms that request information from the lead before they can view the video. Place those forms where you want with Moror A.I.'s intuitive landing page builder. Obtain more information on existing leads and add new leads to your database for further nurturing.

Performance Tracking

Once your video email has been sent, you can maintain a view into how well it performs. Take advantage of robust tools for analyzing email sends and keep track of important metrics such as emails delivered, emails opened, number of video views, and more.
